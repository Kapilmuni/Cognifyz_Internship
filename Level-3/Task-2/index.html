About the Internship

Our Web Developer Internship program is designed to give you hands-on experience in web development. Over the course of one month, you will work on real projects, learn from experienced mentors, and enhance your skills. You will be part of a dynamic team working on innovative solutions, with opportunities to contribute to meaningful projects.

The internship is structured to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of web development, from front-end to back-end, including best practices in coding, design, and project management.

Highlights

  • Gain real-world experience by working on live projects.
  • Receive mentorship from experienced developers.
  • Enhance your portfolio with real work.
  • Network with industry professionals.
  • Flexible work hours and remote work options.

Qualifications

We are looking for candidates with the following skills and technologies:

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Familiarity with front-end frameworks such as React or Angular.
  • Basic understanding of back-end technologies like Node.js or Django.
  • Eagerness to learn and adapt to new technologies.
  • Good problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
